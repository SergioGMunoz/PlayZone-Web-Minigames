What is PlayZone?

PlayZone is a project developed by three young students — Raquel Olmedo, Sergio Muñoz, and David Forero — from the 1st year Web Development Program (DAWE). Its purpose is to put into practice the skills they have learned in web development.

Although our experience is limited, we aim to offer a series of games that are fun and accessible to any user.

For more technical information, you can consult the project documentation.
